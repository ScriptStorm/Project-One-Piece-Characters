Tony Chopper

This small reindeer gained the ability to change its form and think like humans after eating the Hito Hito no Mi fruit. Chopper is a valuable member of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, serving as the ship's doctor. He plays a crucial role in keeping the crew healthy and is known for his medical expertise and kind-hearted nature.

Roronoa Zoro

Zoro was the first pirate (or second member if we count Luffy) to join the Straw Hat Pirates crew. He accepted Luffy's invitation after the captain saved his life. Zoro's formidable skills as a swordsman make him an indispensable asset to the crew, and his dedication to his captain and crewmates is unwavering.

Monkey D. Luffy

Known as 'Straw Hat Luffy,' he is the protagonist of the anime and the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates crew. From a very young age, his greatest dream has been to one day find the legendary treasure of Gol D. Roger and become the new King of the Pirates. Luffy's unyielding determination and adventurous spirit drive him on his journey, inspiring his crew to chase their dreams alongside him.

Sanji

Also known as 'Black Leg' Sanji, this pirate was the fifth to join Luffy's crew. His actions as a pirate have earned him the third highest bounty among the Straw Hat crew, as well as his role as the group's official cook. Sanji's exceptional culinary skills and combat prowess make him an essential member of the crew, providing delicious meals and support in battles.
